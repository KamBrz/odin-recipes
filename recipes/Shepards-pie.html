Cottage pie

Created by the one and only Shepard. This pie rocks in colder weather and it's and absolute comfort food. Also, it's a really simple process to follow, so why not try it.

There's nothing quite like a moist, steaming, portion of beef mixed up with carrots, peas, celery, onion and garlic, cooked in a beef broth mixed with ale. It's all tucked neatly away under cheesy and buttery potato mash mmmmmmmm.

Ingredients:

  • Beef
  • Carrots
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Celery
  • Green peas
  • Beef stock
  • Ale
  • Potatoes
  • Butter
  • Milk
  • Cheese

Steps:

  1. Boil and mash potatoes with cheese
  2. Sear beef
  3. Cook Carrots, Celery, Onion and Garlic
  4. Combine meat and veg and pour over some stock with ale and reduce
  5. Fill a baking tray with meat & veg stew and cover with mash
  6. Bake in the over for 1 hour
