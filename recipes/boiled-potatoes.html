Boiled Potatoes

Ah, boiled potatoes, the staple of every family's life. After boiling you can also mash em to get mashed potatoes, a more fancy version if you add some milk and butter mind you.

Potatoes have a rich heritage and come from the ground, so please make sure to either peel them, or wash them before boiling!

Ingredients

  • potatoes
  • water
  • salt
  • (optional)butter
  • (optional)milk

Steps:

  1. Peel/wash potatoes
  2. Chop potatoes to smaller chunks
  3. Rinse your chopped potatoes
  4. Put them in cold water and add salt
  5. Boil them till fork tender
  6. (optional) Add butter and milk and mash
