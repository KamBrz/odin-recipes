Lasagne recipe

Lasagne is an italian pasta dish with some cream or some shit added to it. The main difference between Lasagne and pasta is that Lasagne comes with flat pasta sheets.

Lasagne was first invented when an Italian worker left his freshly made pasta stuck under his books for too long making it the first pasta sheet. After that, its all history! This is his authentic recipe, Giorgio's recipe

Ingredients:

  • Sheet pasta
  • Canned tomatoes
  • Minced Beef
  • Minced Pork
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Concentrated Tomato Paste
  • Mozzarella Cheese

Steps:

  1. Pre-boil your pasta sheets so they're soft, but not cooked
  2. Cook the onions and garlic
  3. Remove the onion and garlic and sear the meat on the pan
  4. Add Onions, Garlic, Canned tomatoes & paste to the same pot and cook under cover
  5. Layer the cooking sheet staring from pasta at the bottom, pasta, meat sauce, pasta, meat sauce, pasta meat sauce
  6. Pop some cheese on the top you fat bastard
  7. Cook that shit in the over for a bit, till it's done
